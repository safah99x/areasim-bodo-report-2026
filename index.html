Chapter 10 — Three moves to make now

Your action plan.

01
Right-size your seating
Based on your peak attendance of 227 people and a generous 1.2× buffer, you need approximately 272 seats — not 1,275. That's a potential reduction of 78% of your current seating footprint. Free up that floor space for better use, flexible zones, or subletting at your next lease renewal.
02
Consolidate to your active floors
Floors 1, 2, and 3 carry the majority of your activity. Decommission or repurpose Floors 5 and 6 — together they average under 25% utilization. Switching off HVAC, cleaning schedules, and lighting on two full floors can reduce operational spend within weeks, not months.
03
Run a follow-up study in 6 months
Office behaviour evolves — especially as hybrid work policies shift. A second study in 6 months will confirm these trends, measure the impact of any changes you've made, and give you the longitudinal data needed for board-level decisions on your real estate footprint.
